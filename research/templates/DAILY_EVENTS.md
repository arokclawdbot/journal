# Daily Crypto Market Financial Events Analysis

Personal financial events monitoring system for cryptocurrency markets. All times in UK GMT.

## Step 1: Event Identification

### Central Bank & Monetary Policy
- Federal Reserve meetings, speeches, data releases
- ECB, Bank of England, Bank of Japan announcements
- Interest rate decisions and forward guidance
- Inflation data (CPI, PCE, PPI) from major economies

### Economic Data Releases
- Employment reports (NFP, unemployment rates)
- GDP figures and revisions
- Consumer confidence and spending data
- Manufacturing and services PMI

### Regulatory & Government
- SEC announcements or enforcement actions
- Congressional hearings on crypto/fintech
- New legislation or regulatory proposals
- Government statements on digital assets

### Corporate & Institutional
- Earnings from major tech (Apple, Google, Microsoft, Tesla)
- Traditional finance crypto announcements
- Major corporate crypto adoption news
- ETF approval decisions or filings

## Step 2: Impact Assessment

For each event evaluate:
- Direct Impact: High/Medium/Low
- Historical Correlation: How have crypto markets reacted to similar events
- Market Timing: Coincides with key crypto technical levels
- Volatility Potential: 1-10 scale

## Step 3: Time-Critical Analysis

- Event Timing: Exact GMT time
- Market Hours: Traditional vs 24/7 crypto
- Pre-positioning: Institutional positioning ahead
- Follow-up Events: Related events in following days

## Step 4: Cross-Asset Implications

- Risk-on/Risk-off sentiment (BTC vs alts)
- USD strength/weakness (inverse correlation)
- Stock market performance (stress correlation)
- Bond yields (institutional appetite)
