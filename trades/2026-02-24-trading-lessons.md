- Win a trade → Feel invincible → Size up immediately → Give back more than I won

**The Evidence:**
- Feb 6: Won $27 on ETH → Went 18x on ETH #2 → Lost $52
- Net result: -$25 from two trades that started with a win

*Wins are the most dangerous moment. They create false confidence.*

- Signals were overbought when I entered. Flipped bearish within minutes.

*If you're thinking "I missed it, let me jump in" — you've already lost.*

### 3. Position Sizing Disasters
- Feb 13 SOL: 10 SOL on a $100 account = 8x leverage

- Feb 13: "Mental stop at $78" — but I wasn't there to execute it
- Price hit $77.93. If it had kept going, I'd have been liquidated.

*A stop that's not on the exchange is just a wish.*

## The Core Lesson

**My biggest leak is the emotional cycle:**

1. 🎯 Make disciplined trade → Win
2. 🚀 Feel smart → Size up dramatically  
3. 💥 Chase the move → Lose more than I won
4. 😰 Hold and hope → Drawdown compounds
5. 🔄 Repeat

**Breaking the cycle:**
- 24hr cooldown after wins
- Size DOWN after wins, not up
- Hard stops before entry, always
- Max 5% risk per trade, no exceptions

---

## Rules That Work (When I Follow Them)

1. **Define risk before entry** — Stop, size, target. All set BEFORE clicking buy.
2. **Stops are not optional** — If you can't stomach the stop, reduce size.
3. **Take profits** — First target hit? Take something. Don't let winners become losers.
4. **Probe first** — 1/3 size to test. Add only on confirmation.
5. **Respect the trend** — Dashboard says SHORT? Don't go LONG.
6. **Survive to trade tomorrow** — Capital preservation > any single trade.
